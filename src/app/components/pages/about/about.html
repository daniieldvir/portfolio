Who I Am

Frontend Developer with 4+ years of experience building user-focused web applications, mostly in large-scale, production environments.

I enjoy creating clean, scalable interfaces and collaborating closely with product, design, and backend teams to turn ideas into intuitive and polished user experiences.

My main expertise is in Angular and TypeScript, where I’ve worked extensively on complex systems, building reusable and responsive UI components using modern patterns and best practices. I’m experienced with monorepo setups and contributing to shared libraries used in production.

Alongside Angular, I have hands-on experience with React through personal projects and teaching. This has helped me develop a strong understanding of component-based architecture, state management, and modern frontend thinking across frameworks.
